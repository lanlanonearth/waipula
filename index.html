Waipula Village is the only demonstrative village project for the United Nations (UN) Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in China. Utilizing social innovation models, Gunong rural development team wishes to empower existing residents while introducing new residents, so that Waipula Village can become a sustainably peaceful place for the body and mind where new and old villagers thrive.
